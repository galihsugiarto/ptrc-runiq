# RUNIQ GLOBAL PRIVACY POLICY & DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (2026.06)

*Effective Date: June 23, 2026*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. INTRODUCTION

This Global Privacy Policy and Data Processing Agreement ("Policy")
describes how \[PT RUNIQ TEKNOLOGI INDONESIA\] ("RUNIQ", "we", "us", or
"our") collects, uses, processes, and shares your personal data when you
use our RUNIQ sport training and telefitness application and services
(the "App" or "Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy
and handling your data in a transparent and secure manner, in compliance
with global data protection laws, including the General Data Protection
Regulation (GDPR) \[1\], the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
\[2\], and Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law (UU PDP No. 27 of
2022) \[3\].

By using the RUNIQ App, you acknowledge that you have read and
understood this Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please
do not use the App.

## 3. TYPES OF PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT

We collect various types of personal data to provide and improve our
Services. This data is categorized based on its sensitivity and purpose:

### 3.1. GENERAL PERSONAL DATA

- **Identity Data:** Name, username, email address, date of birth,
  gender, profile picture.
- **Contact Data:** Email address, phone number (if provided).
- **Account Data:** Encrypted password, account settings, preferences.
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the App, including
  features accessed, time spent, interactions with other users, and
  content viewed.
- **Marketing and Communications Data:** Your preferences in receiving
  marketing from us and your communication preferences.

### 3.2. SPECIFIC/SENSITIVE PERSONAL DATA (HEALTH DATA)

Due to the nature of a fitness application, we collect and process
sensitive health-related data. This data receives enhanced protection
under GDPR (Article 9), CCPA, and UU PDP. \* **Fitness Data:** Workout
history, activity type (running, cycling, etc.), duration, distance,
pace, calories burned, elevation, training load, recovery metrics. \*
**Biometric Data:** Heart rate, heart rate variability, sleep patterns,
body weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass, VO2 max, blood pressure
(if manually entered or synced from devices). \* **Health Status Data:**
Self-reported health conditions, injury history, dietary preferences,
medical notes (if provided by you). \* **Precise Geolocation Data:** GPS
coordinates collected during outdoor activities to map your routes.
*This is considered sensitive in some jurisdictions.*

**Explicit Consent for Sensitive Data:** We will always obtain your
explicit, affirmative consent before collecting or processing your
Sensitive Personal Data. You have the right to withdraw this consent at
any time via your App settings.

### 3.3. TECHNICAL DATA

- **Device Data:** Device ID, operating system, hardware model, browser
  type, IP address, mobile network information.
- **Log Data:** Server logs, crash reports, access times, pages viewed,
  and referring URLs.
- **Cookie Data:** Information collected through cookies and similar
  tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy).

## 4. HOW WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL DATA

We collect data through various methods: \* **Directly from You:** When
you register an account, update your profile, manually input workout
data, complete surveys, or contact customer support. \* **From Your
Devices:** Automatically collected when you use the App, including GPS,
accelerometer data, and data synced from wearable devices (e.g.,
smartwatches, heart rate monitors) with your explicit permission. \*
**From Third-Party Integrations:** If you choose to link RUNIQ with
third-party services (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit, Strava, Garmin),
we may receive data from those services with your consent. \* **From
Publicly Available Sources:** Limited data from public profiles if you
choose to make your RUNIQ profile public.

## 6. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with third
parties only in the following circumstances and with appropriate
safeguards:

### 6.1. WITH YOUR CONSENT

We will share your data with third parties when you give us explicit
permission to do so (e.g., linking your RUNIQ account to other fitness
platforms).

### 6.2. SERVICE PROVIDERS

We engage third-party service providers to perform functions on our
behalf, such as hosting, data analytics, payment processing, customer
support, and marketing. These providers are contractually bound to
protect your data and only process it according to our instructions and
applicable data protection laws.

### 6.3. LEGAL REQUIREMENTS AND LAW ENFORCEMENT

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or
governmental regulation, or if we believe in good faith that such
disclosure is necessary to: \* Comply with a legal obligation. \*
Protect and defend the rights or property of RUNIQ. \* Prevent or
investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Services. \*
Protect the personal safety of users of the Services or the public. \*
Protect against legal liability.

### 6.4. BUSINESS TRANSFERS

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or
sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal data may be
transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you via email or a
prominent notice on our App of any such change in ownership or control
of your personal data.

### 6.5. AGGREGATED OR ANONYMIZED DATA

We may share aggregated or anonymized data that cannot reasonably be
used to identify you. This data may be used for research, analysis,
industry trends, and marketing purposes.

## 7. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

RUNIQ operates globally, and your personal data may be transferred to,
stored, and processed in countries outside of your country of residence,
including Indonesia, the United States, and other jurisdictions where
our service providers are located. These countries may have data
protection laws that are different from those in your country.

We implement appropriate safeguards to ensure your data remains
protected during international transfers: \* **Standard Contractual

\* **Adequacy Decisions:** We transfer data to countries recognized by

transfers.

## 8. YOUR DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS

We respect your rights regarding your personal data. Depending on your
jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

### 8.1. RIGHT TO ACCESS (GDPR, CCPA, UU PDP)

You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about
you.

### 8.2. RIGHT TO RECTIFICATION (GDPR, CCPA, UU PDP)

You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or
incomplete personal data.

### 8.3. RIGHT TO ERASURE ("RIGHT TO BE FORGOTTEN") (GDPR, CCPA, UU PDP)

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data under
certain conditions. You can initiate account deletion via **Settings \>
Account \> Delete Account** in the App. All data will be permanently
removed or anonymized within 30 days.

### 8.4. RIGHT TO RESTRICTION OF PROCESSING (GDPR)

You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your
personal data under certain conditions.

### 8.5. RIGHT TO OBJECT TO PROCESSING (GDPR, CCPA)

You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data
under certain conditions, particularly for direct marketing purposes.

### 8.6. RIGHT TO DATA PORTABILITY (GDPR, CCPA)

You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have
collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain
conditions.

### 8.7. RIGHT TO WITHDRAW CONSENT (GDPR, UU PDP)

Where we rely on your consent to process your personal data, you have
the right to withdraw that consent at any time. This will not affect the
lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

### 8.8. RIGHT TO LODGE A COMPLAINT

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in
your country of residence if you believe your data protection rights
have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our DPO at
legal@runiqapp.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable
timeframe, typically within 30 days.

## 9. DATA SECURITY

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect
your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or
destruction. These measures include: \* **Encryption:** Data is
encrypted both in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256). \* **Access
Controls:** Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms are in
place for all systems handling personal data. \* **Regular Audits:** We
conduct regular security audits and penetration testing. \* **Employee
Training:** Our employees receive regular training on data protection
and security best practices. \* **Incident Response Plan:** We have a
comprehensive incident response plan to address any potential data
breaches promptly and effectively.

Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot
guarantee the absolute security of your data. In the event of a data
breach, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities in
accordance with applicable laws.

## 13. DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA) CLAUSES

These clauses apply where RUNIQ acts as a Data Processor on behalf of a
user (e.g., a team coach using RUNIQ to manage their team members' data,
where the coach is the Data Controller).

### 13.1. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

- **Data Controller:** The user (e.g., coach, team administrator) who
  determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.
- **Data Processor:** RUNIQ, which processes personal data on behalf of
  the Data Controller.

### 13.2. INSTRUCTIONS

RUNIQ shall process personal data only on the documented instructions of
the Data Controller, unless required to do so by Union or Member State
law to which RUNIQ is subject; in such a case, RUNIQ shall inform the
Data Controller of that legal requirement before processing, unless that
law prohibits such information on important grounds of public interest.

### 13.3. CONFIDENTIALITY

RUNIQ shall ensure that persons authorized to process the personal data
have committed themselves to confidentiality or are under an appropriate
statutory obligation of confidentiality.

### 13.4. SECURITY OF PROCESSING

RUNIQ shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures
to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including inter
alia as appropriate: \* The pseudonymisation and encryption of personal
data. \* The ability to ensure the ongoing confidentiality, integrity,
availability, and resilience of processing systems and services. \* The
ability to restore the availability and access to personal data in a
timely manner in the event of a physical or technical incident. \* A
process for regularly testing, assessing, and evaluating the
effectiveness of technical and organizational measures for ensuring the
security of the processing.

### 13.5. SUB-PROCESSING

RUNIQ shall not engage another processor without prior specific or
general written authorization of the Data Controller. In the case of
general written authorization, RUNIQ shall inform the Data Controller of
any intended changes concerning the addition or replacement of other
processors, thereby giving the Data Controller the opportunity to object
to such changes.

### 13.6. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

RUNIQ shall, taking into account the nature of the processing, assist
the Data Controller by appropriate technical and organizational
measures, insofar as this is possible, for the fulfillment of the Data
Controller's obligation to respond to requests for exercising the data
subject's rights.

### 13.7. ASSISTANCE TO CONTROLLER

RUNIQ shall assist the Data Controller in ensuring compliance with the
obligations pursuant to Articles 32 to 36 of the GDPR (security of
processing, data breach notification, data protection impact assessment,
and prior consultation) taking into account the nature of processing and
the information available to RUNIQ.

### 13.8. RETURN AND DELETION OF DATA

At the choice of the Data Controller, RUNIQ shall delete or return all
the personal data to the Data Controller after the end of the provision
of services relating to processing, and delete existing copies unless
Union or Member State law requires storage of the personal data.

### 13.9. AUDIT RIGHTS

RUNIQ shall make available to the Data Controller all information
necessary to demonstrate compliance with the obligations laid down in
this Policy and allow for and contribute to audits, including
inspections, conducted by the Data Controller or another auditor
mandated by the Data Controller.
